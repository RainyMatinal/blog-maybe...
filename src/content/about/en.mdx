## What Lives Here

This site collects notes on life and design: a lamp, a journey, a book cover, a product that feels unusually considered, or an interface that makes attention easier. It is not a portfolio and not a publication schedule. It is closer to a slow personal magazine.

## Why Bilingual

Chinese is the primary writing language here, because many of the softer observations begin there. The English versions keep the same intent, but they are not literal copies. They are rewritten so the rhythm can feel natural in another language.
